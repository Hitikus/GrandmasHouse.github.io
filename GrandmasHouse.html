    About Us

    Featuring a garden, a year-round outdoor pool and garden views, GRANDMA´S HOUSE is located in Stankovci. The air-conditioned property is 16.1 km from Biograd na Moru, and guests benefit from complimentary WiFi and private parking available on site.

    The villa has 4 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a flat-screen TV, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a patio with pool views. The villa has a playground. Guests at GRANDMA´S HOUSE can enjoy darts on site, or cycling in the surrounding areas. Zadar is 38.6 km from the accommodation, while Šibenik is 29 km away. The nearest airport is Zadar Airport, 30.6 km from GRANDMA´S HOUSE.

    GRANDMA´S HOUSE has been welcoming guests since January 14, 2021
